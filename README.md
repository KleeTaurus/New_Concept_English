# New Concept English

**New Concept English** by [L. G. Alexander](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_George_Alexander) (published by [Longman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Longman)) is a popular English language textbook teaching British English.  

- First published: **October 30, 1967**  
- Revised edition (prepared for Chinese learners): **1997**

> Described as *“a breath of fresh air”* in which Alexander used a syllabus as a foundation for effective learning and organized the language in ways that students and teachers found stimulating.

---

## Course Components
- **Students' Book**  
- **Teacher's Book**  
- **Cassette set** (multi-purpose texts recorded)  
- **Cassette set** (Repetition drill from Teacher’s Book recorded)

---

## Books

### Book 1: *First Things First*
- 144 lessons (72 are exercises)  
- Focus: **oral English**

### Book 2: *Practice & Progress*
- 96 lessons

### Book 3: *Developing Skills*
- 60 lessons  
- Less need for pattern control and contextualization  
- Students cope with new sentence patterns as they occur

### Book 4: *Fluency in English*
- Initial version: 60 lessons  
- Revised edition for Chinese learners: 48 lessons  
- Materials chosen from various publications  
- 9 lessons selected from *The Listener* magazine
